About/Directions

Bad Saarow

Bad Saarow is a lovely small town on the Scharmützelsee about 70 kilometres from Berlin. It is famous for it's thermal baths and lovely nature. Only a few cities in Germany are allowed to be called "Bad" by law. Because the title of "Bad" implies certain mineral qualities to the water.

Directions

60 minutes from the Berlin main train station. With the S-Bahn to Fürstenberg, change to the Bäderbahn that will take you to the picturesque Bad Saarow train station.
