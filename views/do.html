What to do in Bad Saarow

With a countrywide reputation for its therapeutic thermal springs and its spiritual significance, Bad Saarow has quite an array of good dining opportunities that go from mid-range to high-end dining. Here is a guide of our favourite places to dine or have a drink in a relaxing setting:

Water Sports

There are plenty of opportunities to do water sports at the Scharmützelsee, whether you want to rent equipment or hire a guide to take you sailing, besides sailing you can do kayaking and Stand Up Paddleboarding(SUP)

You can rent equipment for all of these here.

Alternatively you can do a sunset sailing session with a guide, more info here

Biking

The Scharmützelsee is a perfect place to ride a bicycle. So many forests and lakeside treasures to be explored. There are plenty of opportunities to rent bicycles and more. Check out Home of Bikes here.
