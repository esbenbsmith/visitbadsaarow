Gastronomy in Bad Saarow

With a countrywide reputation for its therapeutic thermal springs and its spiritual significance, Bad Saarow has quite an array of good dining opportunities that go from mid-range to high-end dining. Here is a guide of our favourite places to dine or have a drink in a relaxing setting:

Park Café/Theather am See

Seestraße 22, 15526 Bad Saarow

Price: €€ - €€€

Rating on Trip Advisor: 4,5/5

Rating on Google: 4,1/5

With a fabulous view on the lake and a casual cozy atmosphere, the Park Café is a perfect place to enjoy the wonders of Bad Saarow at its fullest potential. With a menu that rotates seasonally, one can find dishes that please any taste. The service is friendly, the quality is good, and the dishes are artistic, enjoy! Check them out here.

Die Bühne

Bahnhofspl. 3, 15526 Bad Saarow

Price: €€ - €€€

Rating on Trip Advisor: 4/5

Rating on Google: 4/5

This is the classic Bavarian style restaurant that will give you big dishes full of sauerkraut, sausages, potatoes, pork chops, and a big stein of beer to go with it. If you're very hungry and ready for some heavy set foods, this is the place to be. Very close to the train station. Check them out here.
