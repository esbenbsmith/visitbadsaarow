Activities

Activities in and around Bad Saarow

Saarow Thermen

Am Kurpark 1, 15526 Bad Saarow

This is considered the main attraction of this charming town, as the name of the town is "Bad", signifying that the water of the local hot springs carries special therapeutic properties. In the case of Bad Saarow, also the mud has special mineral properties. Centrally located, in a big park called "Kurpark", and steps from the train station. More information here.

Water Sports

There are plenty of opportunities to do water sports at the Scharmützelsee, whether you want to rent equipment or hire a guide to take you sailing, besides sailing you can do kayaking, pedalboating and Stand Up Paddleboarding(SUP)

You can rent equipment for all of these here.

Alternatively you can do a sunset sailing session with a guide, more info here

Biking

The Scharmützelsee is a perfect place to ride a bicycle. So many forests and lakeside treasures to be explored. The Dubrower Berge are nearby and wildly popular with hikers and cyclists. Below is the main place to rent bicycles, and we recommend renting mountain bikes, as the hills in the forest are quite fun:

Home of Bikes

Hiking

There are some fantastic lakeside trails and many small locations to experience along the lake. Taking the route from Bad Saarow to Wendisch Rietz comes highly recommended. From there you can take the train back to Bad Saarow, after 13 kilometers walking and a nice meal in Wendisch Rietz.
